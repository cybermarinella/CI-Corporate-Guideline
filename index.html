ASCII

The use of ASCII characters permits to represent the logo inside every kind of textual document or interface. The logo itself is code. "Codice Inutile" means "useless code" andm the most useless code is the one that print itself.

Inner

Figure–ground organization is a type of perceptual grouping which is a vital necessity for recognizing objects through vision. In Gestalt psychology it is known as identifying a figure from the background.
